# Vision

Project Sentinel is a long-term software engineering and research project that evolves alongside my learning journey.

Rather than attempting to solve work evaluation with a single solution, Sentinel is designed as a research platform for exploring different approaches to evaluation, trust, reputation, and human-AI collaboration.

Every version represents meaningful learning rather than simply adding more features.

---

# Core Research Question

> How can software help evaluate submitted work fairly, reliably, and transparently while keeping humans responsible for important decisions?

This question will guide the evolution of every version.

---

# Guiding Principles

- Research before implementation.
- Build simple foundations first.
- Every version must introduce meaningful improvements.
- AI should assist human reviewers, not replace them.
- Document every important engineering decision.
- Prioritize fairness, transparency, and trust.
